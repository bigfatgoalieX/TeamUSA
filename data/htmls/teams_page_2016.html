Bradley Beal was selected as the #3 pick in the First Round of the 2012 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. Bradley Beal signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 6, 2012.

Otto Porter was selected as the #3 pick in the First Round of the 2013 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. Otto Porter signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 8, 2013.

John Wall was selected as the #1 pick in the First Round of the 2010 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. John Wall signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 8, 2010. John Wall signed a multi-year extension with the Washington Wizards on July 31, 2013.

Acquired via Draft Rights Trade

Draft Rights to Kelly Oubre, Jr., the #15 pick in the First Round of the 2015 NBA Draft, were acquired by the Washington Wizards from the Atlanta Hawks on June 25, 2015. Kelly Oubre, Jr. signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 9, 2015.

Acquired via Trade

Jared Dudley was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Milwaukee Bucks on July 9, 2015.

Marcin Gortat was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Phoenix Suns on October 25, 2013. Marcin Gortat re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 10, 2014.

Nene Hilario was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Denver Nuggets on March 15, 2012.

Markieff Morris was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Phoenix Suns on February 18, 2016.

Ramon Sessions was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Sacramento Kings on February 19, 2015.

Acquired via Free Agency

Alan Anderson signed a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 11, 2015.

Jarell Eddie signed a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on December 23, 2015.

Drew Gooden signed a contract with the Washington Wizards on February 26, 2014. Drew Gooden re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on March 8, 2014. Drew Gooden re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on March 18, 2014. Drew Gooden re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 14, 2014. Drew Gooden re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 12, 2015.

J.J. Hickson signed a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on February 25, 2016.

Garrett Temple signed a contract with the Washington Wizards on December 25, 2012. Garrett Temple re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 10, 2013. Garrett Temple re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 28, 2014.

Marcus Thornton signed a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on March 9, 2016.
