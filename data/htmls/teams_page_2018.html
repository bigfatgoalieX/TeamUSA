Bradley Beal was selected as the #3 pick in the First Round of the 2012 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. Bradley Beal signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 6, 2012. Bradley Beal re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 26, 2016.

Otto Porter was selected as the #3 pick in the First Round of the 2013 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. Otto Porter signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 8, 2013. Otto Porter re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 19, 2017.

Tomas Satoransky was selected as the #2 pick in the Second Round of the 2012 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. Tomas Satoransky signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 21, 2016.

John Wall was selected as the #1 pick in the First Round of the 2010 NBA Draft by the Washington Wizards. John Wall signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 8, 2010. John Wall signed a multi-year extension with the Washington Wizards on July 31, 2013. John Wall signed a multi-year extension with the Washington Wizards on July 26, 2017.

Acquired via Draft Rights Trade

Draft Rights to Kelly Oubre, Jr., the #15 pick in the First Round of the 2015 NBA Draft, were acquired by the Washington Wizards from the Atlanta Hawks on June 25, 2015. Kelly Oubre, Jr. signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards on July 9, 2015.

Acquired via Trade

Tim Frazier was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the New Orleans Pelicans on June 21, 2017.

Marcin Gortat was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Phoenix Suns on October 25, 2013. Marcin Gortat re-signed to a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 10, 2014.

Chris McCullough was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Brooklyn Nets on February 22, 2017.

Markieff Morris was acquired in a trade by the Washington Wizards from the Phoenix Suns on February 18, 2016.

Acquired via Free Agency

Ty Lawson signed a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on April 11, 2018.

Ian Mahinmi signed a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 7, 2016.

Jodie Meeks signed a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 11, 2017.

Mike Scott signed a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 7, 2017.

Ramon Sessions signed a contract with the Washington Wizards on February 23, 2018. Ramon Sessions re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on March 5, 2018. Ramon Sessions re-signed to a contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on March 16, 2018.

Jason Smith signed a multi-year contract as a free agent with the Washington Wizards on July 7, 2016.
